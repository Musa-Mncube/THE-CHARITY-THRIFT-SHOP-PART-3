About Us

The Multi charity shop was started twenty five years ago when Averil was particularly moved by the need that less advantaged people had for good quality, affordable, second hand clothes. Today the shop is located in Florida, Goldman street and supports over nine organisations and people just wanting to do good work in their communities. Our clothes and items that are donated by community members are sold and proceeds are collected at the end of the year and distributed among the nine organisations which include the Floroma Old Age Home, Gateway School, Westview Clinic’s Drug and Alcohol Rehabilitation Facility, The Association for Disabled People in Krugersdorp as well as Champions for Change which helps abused children and a few others.
